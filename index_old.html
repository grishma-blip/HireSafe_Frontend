Protecting job seekers from sophisticated recruitment scams

Our Mission

In today's competitive job market, recruitment scams have become increasingly sophisticated, targeting vulnerable job seekers with fake job offers and fraudulent recruitment processes. ScamGuard was developed to combat this growing threat using advanced machine learning technology.

The Problem

Every year, thousands of job seekers fall victim to recruitment scams, losing money and personal information to fraudulent schemes. These scams often appear legitimate, making it difficult for even experienced professionals to identify them.

Our Solution

ScamGuard uses a sophisticated Logistic Regression model trained on thousands of verified job postings to identify patterns and characteristics associated with fraudulent recruitment attempts. Our system analyzes multiple factors to provide accurate risk assessments in real-time.
